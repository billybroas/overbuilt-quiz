🔐 Cyber Security 30% of score
Do you use a password manager for all your accounts? 1Password, Bitwarden, LastPass, etc.
Do you have two-factor authentication on your most important accounts? Email, banking, work tools — at minimum
Is your laptop's hard drive encrypted? FileVault on Mac · BitLocker on Windows
Do you have 2FA backup codes stored somewhere safe? In case you lose your phone and get locked out
🏠 Disaster Preparedness 25% of score
Do you have at least 72 hours of water stored? 1 gallon per person per day — minimum
Do you have a 72-hour supply of non-perishable food? Canned goods, protein bars, shelf-stable meals
Do you have a family communication plan? An out-of-area contact + two local meeting points
Do you have working smoke detectors AND a CO detector in your home? Tested in the last 6 months
📋 Insurance Coverage 25% of score
Do you have renters or homeowners insurance? Covers your belongings, liability, and temporary housing
Does your policy cover earthquakes? In California this is always a separate policy
Do you have life insurance? Ideally 10× your annual income as a baseline
Do you have liability protection — a professional liability (E&O) or umbrella policy? Critical if you're self-employed or a homeowner
🔒 Home & Physical Security 20% of score
Do all exterior doors have deadbolts with reinforced strike plates? Strike plates need 3-inch screws — most come with short ones
Do you have a fireproof safe for important documents? Passports, birth certificates, property deeds, insurance policies
Do you have a security system or cameras on your home? Doorbell cam, exterior cameras, or monitored alarm
Do you have a home inventory — photos or video of your valuables? Required to file a complete insurance claim
